I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the School of Automation Science and Engineering at South China University of Technology, supervised by Prof. Wenxiong Kang. From 2023 to 2024, I was a CSC-funded visiting student at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Ying He. Before that, I received my B.Eng. from the College of Software Engineering at East China University of Technology and my Master's degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing Agricultural University.

My research interests include 3D Vision, Scene Representation Learning, 3D Reconstruction. Recent research focuses on Real-time Rendering and Geometric Surface Optimization.
